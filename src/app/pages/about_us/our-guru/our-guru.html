our guru

Swami Himaval Bhadranand

Swami Bhadraanand is a revered spiritual leader dedicated to preserving and promoting the timeless values of Sanatana Dharma in the modern age. Known for his simplicity, humility, and unwavering commitment to service, he has devoted his life to the welfare of humanity and the advancement of righteousness, compassion, and spiritual awareness.

heritage & legacy

Rooted in the wisdom of ancient India

His Origins

Born in the spiritually rich southern region of India, the land that gave rise to great sages such as Adi Shankaracharya and Sree Narayana Guru, Swami Bhadraanand carries forward a legacy of wisdom, social responsibility, and spiritual enlightenment.

His Vision

His vision extends beyond religious teachings, encompassing social harmony, justice, and the upliftment of underserved communities.

His Mission

Inspired by India's ancient monastic traditions, he continues to work tirelessly for the preservation of the country's spiritual and cultural heritage while advocating for peace, unity, and national well-being.
