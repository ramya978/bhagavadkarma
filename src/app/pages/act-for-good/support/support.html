Sanatana Dharma

The Eternal Roots of Our Nation

Everyone desires the fruits of a tree, but only a few take the initiative to nourish and sustain the roots of it. Our existence is deeply dependent on the roots of the principles of Sanatana Dharma, which guided us for millennia.

We must recognise the truth that the vitality of our culture, values, and identity is linked to preserving the sacred roots of Sanatana Dharma. Even our great nation is built on the foundation of Sanatana Dharma thoughts. If we abandon the sacred principles of Sanatana Dharma that guide us forward, our very existence will be in danger.

The Warning

Preserve the Roots or Wither Away

Just as a tree withers when its roots are severed, so too will our nation falter if we forget the timeless truths that have shaped our civilisation. Only true patriots, those who honour and uphold the principles of Sanatana Dharma, can safeguard its legacy for future generations.

Only through this noble mission will we be able to become strong and ensure the prosperity of our country. Also, this mission is not only to preserve our Sanatana culture, but also it will preserve the soul of our nation too.
