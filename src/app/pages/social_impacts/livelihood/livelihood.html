At Bhagavad Karma, we believe that sustainable livelihoods are the foundation of strong families and thriving communities. Our livelihood initiative is dedicated to empowering individuals with the skills, opportunities, and support they need to become financially self-reliant and lead lives of dignity.

We work with unemployed youth, women, farmers, artisans, individuals with disabilities, and economically disadvantaged communities by promoting skill development, entrepreneurship, vocational training, self-employment, and income-generating opportunities. Through mentorship, financial literacy, market linkages, and community partnerships, we help individuals transform their talents into sustainable livelihoods.

Livelihood for All

Our mission is not just to create jobs but to build confidence, foster resilience, and inspire long-term economic independence. By enabling people to earn with dignity, Bhagavad Karma contributes to reducing poverty, strengthening communities, and creating a future where every individual has the opportunity to prosper.
